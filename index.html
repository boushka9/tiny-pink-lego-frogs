Meet Emma

Emma Boushka is currently enrolled in a six-month Coding Bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin to become a full-stack web developer. She thrives in the fast paced and goal-oriented environment of the bootcamp and values the opportunity to continuously learn and grow.

Outside of Emma's academic performance where she maintained a 4.0 GPA throughout college, she has work experience in a variety of roles that required her to be adaptive, analytic, and creative. Her work as a research assistant and a tutor required her to develop unique teaching methods and work effectively with groups with diverse needs and ablities. She is equipped to create realistic goals and improvement plans. She is most proud of her time spent working for Diagnostic Outpatient Imaging because of the opportunity she had to grow within the company as she took on new roles and responsibilities. Because of this, she values the incredible impact that a communicative, collaborative, and supportive team can have on the quality of work produced.

In her free time, Emma loves to take her dog on walks, rollerblade, and build lego sets. Besides her dog, her pride and joy is her Harry Potter Lego collection that she displays in her home.
